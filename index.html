Hello everyone how are you ? I hope you are in good physical and mental health. Let me introduce myself, my name is Noval Alamsyah Adiputra Santoso and people usually call me noval, even though some call Alamsyah, but you can just call me noval, so you'll be more familiar. I was born twenty years ago on November 3, 2001 to a father named Edi Santoso and a mother named Yayuk Ernawati.
